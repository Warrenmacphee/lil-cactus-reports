⚠️ Critical Flags
🔴 WHOLESALE — Large historical refund hit ($-5,619) drove channel net revenue negative
A single Faire refund of $5,619.06 posted Apr 7 (against an order originally placed earlier; 268 units affected) swung wholesale net revenue to –$2,528.65 for the week. Excluding the outlier, wholesale net was $3,090.41 on 15 new orders (341 units), with 26.7% effective margin and 22.2% effective Faire rate. Recommend confirming with Faire/finance whether the refund is one-time and whether to amortize or take in-period.
🔴 Expected Profit: –$11,450 — Below $4,000 floor
Contribution Amount –$4,972 (CM -30.6%) less $6,478 weekly OpEx = –$11,450. Even excluding the wholesale refund outlier (adds back $5,619), normalized Expected Profit would be approx –$5,831 — still well below the $4,001 marginal threshold. Drivers: heavy ad spend $8,735 (Meta $3,794, Google $2,647) vs. modest DTC net of $7,577.
🔴 DTC Contribution Margin: -19.8% (Target: 30%+) — Paid spend exceeds DTC gross margin
DTC Gross Margin 65.3% on $7,577 net revenue is healthy. But Google + Meta spend ($6,442) exceeds the DTC gross margin ($4,944), leaving CM at -19.8%. The pixel/tag attribution issues flagged in prior weeks remain — Google's reported $30k conv value vs. $7.6k actual DTC revenue suggests meaningful overcounting, especially from PMAX. Re-run tag audit before further DTC scale decisions.
🔴 Meta ROAS: 1.85x (Target: 5.0x · Floor: 3.0x) — Below floor
Meta spent $3,794 on 74 reported purchases worth $7,019. "EC | Adv+ Catalog | Feb 23 | Scale 1" was the dominant spender at $1,500+ across the week. Retargeting DPA showed $40+ CPMs late-week with weak ROAS. iOS14 attribution gap remains a known issue.
🟢 Google Reported ROAS: 11.45x — Above target, but verify
Google attributed $30,322 on $2,647 spend (11.45x). PMAX | Spring 2026 alone reported ~$20k conv value on $1,400 spend (~14x). Reported value ($30,322) is ~4x actual DTC net revenue ($7,577), suggesting overcounting of view-through and/or lifetime-modeled conversions. Treat as directionally positive but tag audit still needed before pulling more budget.
🟡 Amazon SP Overall ACOS: 25.8% (Target <20% · Flag >30%)
Amazon SP spent $2,294 attributing $8,877 (ROAS 3.87x). Several campaigns over the 30% ACOS flag — e.g. "Branded Keywords" 82.0%, "Lil Cactus Premium ST Branded" 61.1%, "All Rompers SP DAT" 86.3%, "Baseball Smocked Bishop Dress" 65.8%. ℹ️ Wholesale (ex-outlier): 15 new Faire orders, $3,090 net, no ad spend, 26.7% GM
